In this episode Bryan chat's with Terrisa Bukovinac, founder of ProlifeSF and President of Democrats for Life. Terrisa Bukovinac is a secular anti-abortion activist who has devoted her life to raising awareness about what she regards as an ongoing atrocity. In this conversation, we talk about the ethical underpinnings of her perspective, the story of how she got there, and the current landscape of the pro-life movement.
