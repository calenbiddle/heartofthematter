In this episode, we talk to Amy Mosely, the founder of [Influence Texas](https://www.influencetexas.com/), an organization aimed at using public data to bring transparency to Texas politics. Our conversation covers wrestling with southern identify, corruption, the role of data in political decision-making, and engaging across political boundaries.
